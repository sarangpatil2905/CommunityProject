Solutions

Adaptive Traffic Light SystemAdaptive traffic lights use real-time data to adjust the timing of lights based on current traffic conditions. This can significantly reduce waiting times and improve traffic flow. Learn more about how adaptive traffic lights can benefit our community.

Carpool and Ride-Sharing ProgramsEncouraging carpooling and ride-sharing can decrease the number of vehicles on the road, leading to less congestion and lower emissions. Discover how you can participate in local carpool programs.

Pedestrian and Cyclist-Friendly InfrastructureCreating safe pathways for pedestrians and cyclists can promote walking and biking, reducing the reliance on cars. Explore our plans for new bike lanes and pedestrian crossings.

Our Goals

  • Reduce Traffic Congestion: Implement adaptive traffic light systems to improve traffic flow and reduce congestion.
  • Enhance Safety: Develop strategies to minimize accidents and ensure the safety of pedestrians and drivers.
  • Promote Sustainable Transport: Encourage the use of public transport, cycling, and walking to reduce the number of vehicles on the road.
  • Educate the Community: Raise awareness about traffic rules and the benefits of smart traffic management.

3-Way RoadCross

Static Mode

operate on pre-set, fixed schedules. The signal timings (green, yellow, and red phases) are determined based on historical data or standard traffic flow patterns, and they remain constant regardless of real-time traffic conditions.

Adaptive Mode

adapt signal timings in real-time based on current traffic conditions. These systems use various sensors and data analytics to optimize traffic flow, reduce congestion, and improve overall efficiency.
